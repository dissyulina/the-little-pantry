How To Shop

Who we are

Packaging waste is a problem worldwide - tons of plastic winds up in our ocean destroying the sea life, and on earth plastic takes the longest time to decompose compared to other materials. That’s why we are here as a plastic-free grocery shop where we sell everything in bulk system. You can bring your own containers from home, and refill it at our shop as much as you need. Together we can make a significant impact to do better for our mother earth!

“We don’t need a handful of people doing zero waste perfectly.
We need millions of people doing it imperfectly.”

Anne Marie Bonneau, The Zero Waste Chef

Why shop with us

Plastic-Free

We use our bulk bin system made of wood and glass for all of our products. Bring your own containers from home, or buy our own glass jar or recycled food bag, and fill them up.

Reduce Food Waste

Buy only how much you need, with minimum weight of 10 grams. This prevents food spilling from unused food item.

Organic Products

82% of our products are organic. We are striving to give the healthier choice to our costumer.

Comparable Prices

Because we cut out the packaging cost, we are able to give our customers comparable prices to the supermarket, or even cheaper. And because you only buy the amount that you need, you are reducing your overal spending on food.
